Instructions

Snake and Ladder is a classic board game for 2 or more players. Players take turn rolling a die and moving their game piece along a numbered grid.


The goal is to reach the last square, usually numbered 100, by exact count. If a player lands on a square with a ladder, they climb to a higher-numbered square. If they land on a square with a snake, they slide down to a lower-numbered square.


The game continues until one player reaches the final square, making him as the winner.
